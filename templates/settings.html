Help

What TradeHub Does

TradeHub Markets runs party docking, local market stock, buy and sell transactions, cargo tracking, shipyard purchases, consumable restock, repairs, TradeHub capital, news tickers, and vehicle combat tools from one module.

Compendiums

Trade goods, restock supplies, vehicle consumables, and shipyard craft all come from compendium packs. Select a pack in the Compendiums tab, then optionally choose a folder or subfolder when you want only part of that pack used.

Markets

Docking generates or refreshes the current location market. Prices, stock, illegal-market state, emergency state, last-paid prices, and predictive rumours are stored in TradeHub world data so players see the same market.

Vehicles

Starport Services lists world vehicle actors the player owns. TradeHub reads equipment and weapon modules on each vehicle to calculate HP, max HP, AC, module value, jump data, repair state, cargo, and combat targets.

Capital

TradeHub Capital is internal party money. Purchases, sales, restock, repairs, shipyard buys, banking adjustments, and GM full-service repair all update this value without needing a Bank of Holding actor.

Repairs And Pristine

Make Pristine fully restores modules and recalculates final vehicle stats. Live sync updates structural stats when modules are added, removed, or edited while preserving damage. Full Service Repair bills capital; Ability Check Repair applies rolled HP.

Combat Damage

The Combat Damage tool reads recent chat roll data but keeps fields editable. Shields absorb first, hull reinforcement protects after shields, fuel scooping targets the fuel scoop, mining targets refinery first, and module AC limits vulnerable targets.

News Tickers

Create folder TradeHubMarkets, journal TradeHubNews, and pages named after locations. Each line can become a ticker headline. Predictive rumours can add hidden market hints when enabled.

GM Controls

The floating GM bar opens Docking, Starport Services, Ship Tools, Combat Damage, Banking, and Settings. Player macros are bundled in the TradeHub Markets Macros compendium for quick access.
